Take Your Action by Becoming a:

Malaysia Youth Volunteer (MYV) is an Engagement with AIESEC initiative run by AIESEC Malaysia for Malaysian youth. The program empowers young people to take ownership of their personal development and at the same time take action towards solving social issues in Malaysia through volunteering in different SDG-aligned projects.

Volunteering Duration 4 Weeks/ 28 Days
Expected Working Hours 10 - 15 Hours per week
Mode of volunteering Physical / Online

Frequently Asked Question

What will I do in volunteering project ?

Step out of your comfort zone and volunteer in a challenging environment with across-Malaysia & international exposure to develop leadership qualities and interpersonal skill through participating in interactive workshops, project activities and our unique Leadership Development Model.

How will MYV projects help me develop leadership skills?

MYV projects allow you to work in diverse teams to carry out tasks. Through working with different youths and carrying out various tasks, you will learn to develop interpersonal skills such as communication skills, teamwork, problem-solving and much more.

How can I create an impact through the volunteering project?

Each project is aligned with a Sustainable Development Goal (SDG). Through the virtual projects, you will be taking steps towards solving a social issue by becoming aware of the reality of the situation, educating others and contributing to the solution.

Do I need any skills to join a project?

MYV projects are aimed to develop youths from diverse backgrounds so no specific skills are needed to join a project. Any knowledge required to execute the project will be taught to all volunteers during the project.
