HISTORY

BETTER WORLD is an educational website that aims to raise the awareness of today's society towards Sustainable Development Goals and provide useful educational information.It was found by a student from Sunway University in the year of 2022 for their Web Fundamentals Final Project purpose. The name of the website "BETTER WORLD" has the meaning where every little step the society took helps to create a world that is better than today. HOPE everyone will like it !!(>V<)
